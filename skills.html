My UX/UI Skills

Empathy

I believe listening is the key to empathy, and being able to translate user pain points into thoughtful iterations. I always keep users at the forefront of my design process with user personas.

User Research

To obtain quantitative data, I use Google Forms and Instagram Polls to get a quick census on overall information. I obtain qualitative information through user interviews.

User Interviews

As a trained journalist, I am skilled at interviewing. I use lengthened pauses to try and get the user to fill in silence with more information. I ask "Why" questions to get to the root of user pain points."
